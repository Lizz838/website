Welcome to planet A-1618 c.

After contact with the cosmos is mysteriously cut, a lone AI observes as an otherworldly ecosystem blooms in humanity's absence. This is the story of Icarus Flew, a speculative biology and worldbuilding excercise focusing on a partially-terraformed world inhabited only by microbes.
